Battlesquares Team Project:


As our first team project at Epicodus we used HTML CSS Javascript and JQuery to create a game similar to Battleship.

Rules:

  • Determine with your opponent a set number of squares to pick.
  • Don't look at the screen when your oponent is selecting their squares.
  • Take turns selecting squares, first person to select allo their opponents squares wins.

Instructions to start:

  • Click Player vs Player or VS Computer to begin.
